Our Journey

Founded in 1995, AARAM began as a small workshop with a big dream: to create furniture that combines timeless design with exceptional craftsmanship. Our founder, John Smith, started with a simple vision - to transform houses into homes with beautiful, functional furniture.

Over the years, we've grown from a local workshop to a renowned furniture brand, but our commitment to quality and attention to detail remains unchanged. Each piece we create tells a story of dedication, passion, and the pursuit of perfection.

Today, we continue to honor our heritage while embracing innovation, creating furniture that stands the test of time and brings comfort and style to homes around the world.
