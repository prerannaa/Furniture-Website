About Kumbha Group Pvt. Ltd.

With over 5 years of experience, Kumbha Group Pvt. Ltd. has established itself as a pioneer and trendsetter in the manufacturing and supply of furniture for hotels, restaurants, schools, and offices in Nepal.

Our founder, Mr. Bidhan Koirala, a management graduate and first-generation entrepreneur, recognized a gap in Nepal’s contract furniture industry and introduced a “one-stop solution” for sturdy, customized, and standardized furniture. His vision, combined with a commitment to quality, professionalism, after-sales service, and timely nationwide delivery, has positioned Kumbha Group as a trusted name across multiple sectors.

Our state-of-the-art factory in Bharatpur, Chitwan, has a monthly production capacity of 3,000 chairs and tables. We specialize in a wide range of customized chairs, tables, sofas, booth seating, and banquet furniture designed for restaurants, cafés, hotels, and event spaces. Additionally, we manufacture school furniture, office chairs, desks, cabinets, and modular office solutions to meet the needs of educational institutions and corporate spaces. Our exclusive range also includes wood and metal chairs, tables, tabletops, table bases, and hotel trolleys.

Through our flagship brand Aaram, we deliver furniture that embodies comfort, durability, and luxury. Our products are supplied to all major cities across Nepal and exported to India. Today, Aaram furniture is trusted by leading international and Nepali hotel chains, schools, and offices for its premium quality and innovative designs.
